About 讀文 duwen

duwen is a free reader for public-domain Chinese literature, built for learners: tap any word for pinyin and definitions, color words by HSK level, read in traditional or simplified script, and export the words you save to Anki. There are no accounts — your reading position and vocabulary live in your own browser.

Why these texts are free

Every work here is verified public domain in both China and the United States. Lu Xun died in 1936: his works entered the Chinese public domain fifty years after his death, and because he died before 1946 their US copyrights were never restored under the URAA. Each work page states its public-domain basis and text source.
